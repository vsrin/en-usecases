What enCode solves

Institutional knowledge encoded, not recalled.

Appetite rules, jurisdiction requirements, and SOPs live as versioned, governed definitions — not floating in model weights where they can drift, hallucinate, or become unauditable. Every AI decision traces back to a specific version of the rule that was in effect at the time of the decision.

01

Encode once, apply everywhere

Rules encoded in enCode are consumed by any workflow, tool, or agent that needs them. One definition. Consistent application across every pipeline.

02

Versioned to the decision

When a regulator asks what rule governed this decision, enCode answers with the exact version, effective date, and governing content that was active at the time.

03

Deterministic, not probabilistic

Appetite, exclusions, and jurisdiction rules don't belong in model weights. enCode routes them through deterministic lookup — zero chance of hallucination on governed facts.
